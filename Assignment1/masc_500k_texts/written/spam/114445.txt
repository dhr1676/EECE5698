 We have a five step plan to help you succeed:                                                                     
 ------------------------------------                                                                     
                                                                     
 1. Switch to IC Plus Pricing: Also known as pass thru pricing.                                                                     
 This pricing plan offers the lowest cost per transaction no                                                                     
 matter which card type.                                                                     
                                                                     
 2. Know your average transaction: Visa/MasterCard has special                                                                     
 programs for high and low ticket transactions that can save                                                                     
 you money. Also, pin debit can save you money if your                                                                     
 average transaction is high enough.                                                                     
                                                                     
 3. Know your card mix. What type of cards does your business                                                                     
 normally accept, business, rewards, consumer cards? Visa/                                                                     
 MasterCard has special programs for special card types.                                                                     
 Your processor should be able to tell you this information.                                                                     
                                                                     
 4. Review your statement every month. Five minutes of review                                                                     
 can save you hundreds of dollars in unnecessary fees. Banks                                                                     
 may increase your rates or add on fees with a simple notice                                                                     
 in monthly statement. When that occurs, call your processor                                                                     
 and challenge those charges. Many times they can be waived.                                                                     
                                                                     
 5. Have a credit card processing analysis done at least once a                                                                     
 year. Be prepared to switch to a better deal.
